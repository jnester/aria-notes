# Supply Chain Management Software - Requirements

*Captured: 2026-02-05*

## Data Import
- Upload existing artifacts (spreadsheets, current processes)
- Upload orders submitted to manufacturers (with part details)
- Auto-generate order templates from historical orders

## Core Management
- Suppliers per part (which suppliers provide which parts)
- Available inventories (stock levels)
- Manufacturers (who makes what)

## Product Structure (BOM)
- Products you produce
- Parts/items that comprise each product (bill of materials)
