## Key Plot Points So Far

### Setting
- Future Earth divided among 5 mega-corporations that replaced governments
- Prodigy (run by "The Boy Genius") and Yutani are two of them
- The Boy Genius operates from a secret island lab called "Neverland"

### The Hybrids (Peter Pan Theme)
- Boy Genius spent billions and a decade creating hybrids—human consciousness merged with synthetic AI bodies
- Only works with children (adult consciousness rejects the merge)
- Recruits terminally ill kids, transfers them to adult synthetic bodies that never age
- Named after Lost Boys from Peter Pan: Wendy is the first and leader
- Led by a 300-year-old white-haired synth

### Main Characters
- **Wendy:** First hybrid. Was a bald little girl, now in adult synthetic body. Has a brother she watches through hacked cameras. Can hear/speak alien language.
- **The Boy Genius:** Young founder (teen/early 20s), casual but ruthless, curious above all, treats damaged hybrids as "IT issues"
- **Wendy's Brother:** Search and rescue, now has artificial lung, forced to stay and work for Prodigy
- **White-haired Synth:** 300 years old, stoic, performs alien autopsies

### Alien Plot
- Yutani ship returning from 65-year deep space mission crashed in Prodigy territory
- Carried alien specimens including xenomorphs
- One xenomorph escaped, killed people, laid eggs
- Prodigy captured 5 alien specimens, now studying them while lawyers fight Yutani
- White-haired synth extracted embryo from facehugger, birthed baby alien
- Wendy was singing to it—baby alien imprinted on her as mother
