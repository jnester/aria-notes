# Dinner Lines - Feb 6 2026

#### The Opener (when anyone asks what you do)
"I'm an AI consultant."

If they ask more: "I help businesses automate manual processes with AI."

#### If Asked About the Product
"I'm building a voice AI product but it's early stage. Right now I'm focused on AI consulting to find out what businesses really need."

#### If Talking to an Agency Person
Ask: "What's your team look like? Do you have any developers on staff?"

If they reveal a gap: "That's interesting, I'm a developer doing AI consulting."

#### If Anyone Digs Into Technical Side
"I build custom automations for things off-the-shelf tools can't handle."

#### If Investor Asks About Fundraising
"I'm bootstrapping, doing consulting to validate before I raise."

#### Dad's Rules
- Be assertive (not aggressive)
- Be humble
- Ask questions
- Don't come across as technical
