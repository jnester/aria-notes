## Why You Never Start Relationships

The full chain: start relationship → might get exploited → would have to end it → don't know how → could make an enemy → so never start.

Perfect defense. Perfect isolation.

**The real fear isn't connection. It's exploitation dressed up as connection.**

Your alarm bells aren't wrong - they're just miscalibrated. Trained on bad data from people who took without giving.

---

## The Vacuum of Isolation

When no one's watching, no one to disappoint, no stakes... things slip. Habits form. Standards drop. Because why not?

**You're not broken. You're just unsupervised. And you're not meant to be.**

---

## The Fix Isn't Perfection

You don't have to become perfect before you let anyone in. That's just another way to stay alone forever.

Connection naturally pulls you toward who you want to be:
- You won't binge as hard when you have dinner plans
- You won't let the house slide when someone might come over
- It's not shame - it's just having a reason to show up for yourself
